Below you can listen to curated unconditional audio samples generated by different diffusion models. All architectures are configured with around 40 million parameters for fair comparison.

Abstract

This work presents MR-CQTdiff, a novel multi-resolution Constant-Q Transform (CQT) architecture for diffusion-based audio generation. Our approach leverages the octave-based structure of the CQT to process audio at multiple resolutions simultaneously, enabling more efficient and high-quality audio synthesis compared to traditional approaches.
